CARE AND HEALTH FOR
YOUR CACTUS

  • In cold times, add water once a moth and during the summer do it when the soil is very dray.

  • Have good drainage so that the cactus does not accumulate water.

  • Place on your cactus or succulent in a location with indirect light.

  • Do not water more then necessary, as it can accumulate too much water and the plant will rot

  • Do not expose to hight temperatures, as succulent plants are not fans of extremes.
